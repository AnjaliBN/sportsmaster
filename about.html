Welcome to Sports Master!

Sports Master is the exclusive distributor of synthetic/wood sport surface and equipments. We provide parts and service support for leading worldwide brands in the commercial, light commercial and residential segments of the sport coating market.

We have achieved over a 20% market share in the IT Company, resort and the high-end recreational club markets in this region. Sports Master represents, and is the exclusive applicator for the sports surfacing products from many of the world’s top producers of specialty sports surfaces. We build track and field facilities, artificial grass and hard court, tennis courts, squash courts, and specialty surfaces for gymnasiums and multipurpose activity areas. The company plans and builds these facilities on a turnkey base.

We like to give good quality sports grounds in your esteemed organizations with great care for quality. We are sincere and clear to become an active participant in promotion of all types of synthetic/wood sports surface industry. We want to make more people take on to sports in the country and make it affordable.
