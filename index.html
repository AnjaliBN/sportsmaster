Sports Master

We specialize in the design, development and installation of international standard recreation sports surface and sports facilities. The company provides its products and services to many market sectors including private recreation clubs, educational institutions, hotels and resorts, residential, government and commercial clients.

Operating from the Bangalore base since 1999, Sports Master handles development, marketing and management of recreational and leisure facilities. The company’s consultative approach, world standard products and responsible follow-up have earned it a reputation as the leader in its unique field of business.

We are the exclusive distributors of synthetic/wood sport surface and equipment’s. We are very much interested to work with quality and time bound requirement of sports faculty in India. We excel in the construction of sports infrastructure facility and its logistics materials.
